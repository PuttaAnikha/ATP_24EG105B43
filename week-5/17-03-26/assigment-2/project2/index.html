ChatGPT (by OpenAI)

ChatGPT remains the most versatile AI, acting as a general-purpose conversational assistant for writing, coding, brainstorming, and image generation. It can process multimodal inputs, including text, images, and documents, making it a comprehensive tool for both everyday tasks and complex creative projects.

Claude (by Anthropic)

Claude is regarded for its ability to produce highly coherent, nuanced, and safe text, making it a "thoughtful alternative" to ChatGPT. It excels at coding, summarizing very long documents, and maintaining a natural conversational tone, often preferred for high-stakes creative writing or complex coding tasks.

Perplexity AI

Perplexity is a specialized AI "answer engine" that provides real-time information with direct citations for its sources. Unlike traditional search engines, it eliminates the need to click multiple links by synthesizing search results into a concise summary, making it indispensable for research and fact-finding.
